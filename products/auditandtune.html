Audit and Tune

<> Audit & Tune is a way for CMD to become familiar with a Client's environment. It provides a thorough overview of the environment and documents best practices including optimizations to be made for the PostgreSQL Environment.
Should the Client decide that our recommendations are proper for their environment, CMD will happily make the documented changes during business hours at no extra charge. Pricing includes a single cluster/server.
